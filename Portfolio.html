I am Canadian teenager who likes to make games as a method of learning and practicing software development. I am studying engineering at UBC Okanagan, working towards a degree in electrical engineering with a minor in Computer Science.

I taught myself to make games with GameMaker Studio 2 when I was 13, and later using Unity. Lately I've been doing web development using HTML, CSS and Javascript.

I am also a contributor to Colonist.io , an online multiplayer board game.
