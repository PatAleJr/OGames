River Ravager

River Ravager



April 2021

Arcade

Unity



You’re searching for treasure. It’s rumored to be at the heart of the jungle. But it gets more and more dangerous as you penetrate deeper into the wilderness.

Made in 48 hours for Ludum Dare 48. Theme: Deeper and deeper.

Controls: WASD to move, SPACE to jump, MOUSE to shoot, M to mute
