Wind-Up Ducks

Wind-Up Ducks



August 2020

Real Time Strategy

Unity



Command your ducks to fight enemies. Move with arrows. Wind them up by holding the space bar, awaken them with z and put them to sleep with x.

Tips:
While the ducks are asleep you can push them around. Then awaken them so they attack the enemies close to them. Sleeping ducks deplete their energy much slower than awake ones.

Music: Casual Game BGM by B.G.M on the Unity Asset Store
